**CITATION**: GlobalRPH (2025) - GLP-1 Agonists for NASH Treatment: New Clinical Evidence Reveals Surprising Results
**CONTEXT**:
- Sample: Review of clinical trial data from multiple studies
- Setting: Global clinical trials for NAFLD/NASH treatment
- Period: Recent clinical trials up to 2025
**DATA**:
- Semaglutide demonstrated NASH resolution without worsening of fibrosis in **36 to 59%** of patients with liver fibrosis, compared with **17%** in placebo group
- Retatrutide (triple GIP/GLP-1/glucagon receptor agonist) normalized liver fat in **about 90%** of patients after 48 weeks of treatment
- NAFLD prevalence: **32.4%** in general population
- Up to **15%** of NAFLD patients develop NASH
- Up to **70%** of T2DM patients affected by NAFLD
- One in five adults with obesity and diabetes has NASH with clinically relevant fibrosis
